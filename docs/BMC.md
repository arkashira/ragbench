# Business Model Canvas – **ragbench**

| **Building Block** | **Details** |
|--------------------|-------------|
| **Value Proposition** | • **Zero‑trust, on‑premise RAG evaluation** – guarantees data sovereignty and compliance for enterprises that cannot send proprietary data to third‑party services.<br>• **Schema‑driven JSON validation** – automatic Pydantic‑based checks that ensure model outputs conform to strict contracts, reducing downstream bugs and legal risk.<br>• **Extensible benchmark suite** – plug‑in architecture for custom metrics, datasets, and retrieval back‑ends (vector stores, hybrid search, LLMs).<br>• **Developer‑friendly CLI & reporting dashboard** – one‑command execution, reproducible results, and visual dashboards for fast stakeholder communication.<br>• **Enterprise‑grade security** – containerized, isolated execution with audit logs, role‑based access, and optional air‑gapped deployment. |
| **Customer Segments** | 1. **Large enterprises & regulated industries** (finance, healthcare, government) that require on‑premise AI evaluation.<br>2. **RAG platform providers** (vector DB vendors, LLM SaaS) needing a standardized test harness for their customers.<br>3. **AI research labs & ML Ops teams** building proprietary retrieval‑augmented pipelines.<br>4. **Compliance & security consultancies** that embed evaluation tooling into client engagements. |
| **Channels** | • **GitHub & Open‑Source community** – free starter edition, documentation, and issue tracker.<br>• **Direct sales & professional services** – enterprise licensing, custom integration, and support contracts.<br>• **Technical webinars, workshops, and conference talks** (NeurIPS, KubeCon, AI‑Sec events).<br>• **Partner portals** of vector‑DB and LLM vendors (bundled offering). |
| **Revenue Streams** | 1. **Enterprise License (annual SaaS‑style)** – per‑seat or per‑node pricing for the fully‑featured, support‑backed version.<br>2. **Professional Services** – onboarding, custom metric development, compliance audit assistance.<br>3. **Premium Plugins Marketplace** – vetted metric packs, industry‑specific schemas, and integration adapters (revenue share with contributors).<br>4. **Training & Certification** – paid courses on secure RAG evaluation best practices. |
| **Key Resources** | • **Core codebase** (Python 3.11+, Pydantic, Click, Docker) – maintained in the `axentx/ragbench` repo.<br>• **Domain expertise** – senior RAG engineers, security architects, compliance lawyers.<br>• **Data assets** – curated benchmark datasets (public & partner‑provided) stored in the company’s pgvector knowledge store.<br>• **Infrastructure** – CI/CD pipelines, Docker image registry, and secure build environment.<br>• **Brand & community** – open‑source contributors, conference presence, and Axentx’s BRAIN knowledge graph. |
| **Key Activities** | • **Product development** – extending schema validation, adding new metric plug‑ins, improving CLI UX.<br>• **Security hardening** – regular penetration testing, audit‑log integration, zero‑trust networking.<br>• **Community & partner enablement** – open‑source releases, documentation, SDKs, and joint go‑to‑market with vector‑DB vendors.<br>• **Compliance certification** – ISO‑27001, SOC‑2, and industry‑specific attestations.<br>• **Support & services delivery** – SLA‑backed issue resolution, custom integration work. |
| **Key Partners** | • **Vector‑DB & Retrieval Engine vendors** (e.g., Pinecone, Milvus, Weaviate) – co‑marketing and bundled benchmark suites.<br>• **LLM providers** (OpenAI, Anthropic, Cohere) – reference implementations and API adapters.<br>• **Security firms** – third‑party audits, zero‑trust framework contributions.<br>• **Open‑source ecosystems** – Pydantic, Click, pytest communities for shared tooling.<br>• **Compliance consultants** – help translate regulatory requirements into benchmark schemas. |
| **Cost Structure** | • **R&D salaries** – senior engineers, security experts, compliance specialists.<br>• **Infrastructure** – CI/CD runners, Docker registry, secure build servers, cloud‑agnostic test clusters.<br>• **Community & marketing** – conference sponsorships, webinars, documentation hosting.<br>• **Legal & compliance** – certifications, audit fees, licensing management.<br>• **Support operations** – Tier‑1/2 support staff, ticketing system, SLA monitoring. |
| **Unfair Advantage** | • **Zero‑trust, on‑premise design** that no other open‑source RAG benchmark currently guarantees.<br>• **Tight integration with Axentx’s BRAIN knowledge graph**, enabling rapid creation of domain‑specific schemas and metrics.<br>• **Proven enterprise security pedigree** (ISO‑27001 ready) built on Axentx’s existing compliance framework.<br>• **Marketplace for premium plugins** that creates network effects among vendors and customers. |
